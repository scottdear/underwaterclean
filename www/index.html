Our Services

Underwater Clean
  • Gentle in water hull cleaning with least abrasive materials possible
  • Check for an clean barnacle growth on hull and running gear.
  • Check condition of bottom paint and hull issues like blisters or de-lamination
  • Check and replace zincs (additional charge may apply) to maintain them at no less than 40% (for maximum reliability).
  • Check for negative effects of electolysis and galvanic corrosion on through-hulls, props and running gear
  • A cleaning report that describes any evidence of electrolysis, galvanic corrosion, blisters in hull and/or delamination, status of zincs, and any other recommended maintenance.
